Kangaroo

Cloud-based android mobile phone contact backup system. Secures your contacts and get from anywhere.

Download Download View Kangaroo on GitHub

Welcome to Kangaroo Android App.

This is the small app with great functionality. This app helps to backup phone contacts to cloud and get them back at any time anywhere.

Mobile lost?, No problem!!!. Wait...Buy another mobile and install this app and login with your credentials and get contact back.
